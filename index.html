About.

In the era of technological advancement and pre-occupied busy world, one doesn’t find the time for oneselves. So in order to make one’s leisure good, we present Leisures. Leisures is a platform where one can find everything they want to do in their free time with a built-in suggestion module. So, it analyses what mood an individual is in and makes suggestions on what he/she should do in order to make their free time/leisure good. For example, if someone is gloomy and he opens leisures . It will first analyse the person’s temper and after figuring out his distress , it would suggest him to either listen to sad songs or watch movies and will fetch respective data for it as well.

Our Process

1. Analysing a person’s frame of mind/mood and make suggestions accordingly
2. Fetching the respective data from various sources.
3. Integrated ChatBot.
4. All sorts of entertainment sources on one platform.

Our Goal

Leisures is a platform where one can find everything they want to do in their free time with a built-in suggestion module. So, it analyses what mood an individual is in and makes suggestions on what he/she should do in order to make their free time/leisure good.







The project Leisures is a Progressive Web Application which is supported on browser as well as on phone, just like a native Application. It is the web based project with rest web api’s from different sources, for fetching data of different format. The ML prediction model with integrated chatBot is pretty feasible for a web Application.
